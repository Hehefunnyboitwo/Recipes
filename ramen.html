Chicken Ramen

Description

Ingredients

  • Maruchan Instant Chicken Ramen
  • Water
  • Bowl
  • Microwave

Recipe

  1. Put the dry noodles in the bowl
  2. Add water into the bowl until the noodles are fully submerged
  3. Put the bowl in the microwave and heat it for 3 1/2 to 5 minutes
  4. Once cooked, strain the water and add the sauce packet
  5. Mix the sauce in and then enjoy
